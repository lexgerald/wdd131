Sierra Leone

Sierra Leone is a presidential republic located on the Atlantic Ocean in West Africa. It shares borders with Guinea to the north and east, and Liberia to the south. The country has mangrove swamps along the coast, wooded hills, and a plateau in the interior, with the eastern region being mountainous. The constitution provides for the separation of state and religion and freedom of conscience.
