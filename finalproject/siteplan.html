Wills Pious Coffee Shop

Project Site Plan Document

Site Name

Wills Pious Coffee Shop

This name was selected because:

  • I chose Wills Pious because it combines the idea of expertly crafted (Wills).
  • With a welcoming, cozy atmosphere (Pious).
  • Easy to remember and brandable
  • Domain availability: willspious.com (preferred)

Site Purpose

The Wills Pious website will serve as:

  • To create a warm and inviting online presence.
  • To mirrors the cozy atmosphere of our physical cafe.
  • Making it easy for customers to discover our offerings and visit us.
  • Joining a coffee-loving community.

Scenarios

Target audience questions the site will answer:

  1. Busy Remote Worker: "I need a quiet place to work—do you have WiFi, outlets, and long seating hours?"
  2. Community-Oriented: "Do you host events? I’d love to meet other coffee fans!"
  3. First-time Visitor: "I’m new to the area—what makes Wills Pious different from other coffee shops?"
